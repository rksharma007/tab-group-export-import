# Privacy Policy

**Extension:** Group Tabs – Export & Restore
**Last updated:** 21 July 2026

## Summary

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All
processing happens locally in your browser.

## What the extension accesses

To do its job, the extension reads:

- The URLs and titles of your open tabs.
- The names, colors, and collapsed state of your tab groups.

This information is used only to create the export file you choose to save, and to
recreate your tabs and groups when you import that file.

## What the extension does not do

- It does not send any data to the developer or to any third party.
- It does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.
- It does not make network requests.
- It does not store your browsing data anywhere except inside the export file that
  you explicitly save to your own device.

## Data control and retention

The only copy of your exported data is the file you save. You decide where it is
stored, who it is shared with, and when it is deleted. The extension keeps no
separate copy.

## Permissions

- `tabs` — required to read tab URLs and titles for export and to open tabs on restore.
- `tabGroups` — required to read and recreate tab groups.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised and the new
version published with the extension.
